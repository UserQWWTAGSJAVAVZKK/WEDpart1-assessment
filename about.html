About Us

Our History

The Daily Loaf Bakery started in 2015 as a small home-based business. What began with baking sourdough bread for neighbours has grown into a beloved local storefront. We are proud to be part of this community.

Our Mission

To bring the warmth and comfort of freshly baked, high-quality bread and pastries to our community every day.

Our Vision

To be the heart of the neighbourhood — a place where people come not just for bread, but for a sense of community and a moment of joy.

Our Values

  • Quality ingredients from local farmers
  • Traditional baking methods
  • Warm and friendly service
  • Giving back to our community
